From One Desk to Nationwide Coverage

Founded in 2020 by Ahmed Raza, Drivora started as a single insurance advisory desk in Karachi's PECHS neighbourhood. The mission was simple: help Pakistani vehicle owners find honest, transparent coverage without confusing jargon or hidden fees.

By partnering with Pakistan's top insurers — Zurich, EFU, Adamjee, Jubilee, TPL, and more — Drivora now serves drivers across 20+ cities, processing claims in under 48 hours and offering instant online quotes.
